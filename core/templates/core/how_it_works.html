Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can I hire?

Typically 3-7 days for the first shortlist. Urgent replacements can be handled within 48 hours for active roles.

What if it doesn't work out?

We offer a 100% free replacement guarantee within the first 60 days. We'll find you a new candidate immediately.

Do you handle payroll?

Yes, we can manage global payroll and compliance, so the talent works as a contractor for you but is employed by us/partners locally.
